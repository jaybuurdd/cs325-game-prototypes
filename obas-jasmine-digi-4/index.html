  • Story/Setting/Premise:

    The character is name Owwee and he's a creature from a tribe that lives underground in the grasslands of Yagur. Each year the chosen special child of the tribe must complete a quest of soaking the sacred rock in the "Pond Elixir". This pondlocated in the center of a nearby village in Yagur called Ponx. Ponx has a legend of creatures that if seen will leave a curse on all who are laid eyes on it and their bloodlines. Owwee is the chosen special child and must complete this quest, if spotted it can become very dangerous because it will lead to the hunt, capture, and inevitably death for him. Completing this quest will bring great strength to the tribe and Owwee will inherit the core of that strength if completed and become the protector.
